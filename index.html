How to Play Blackjack

Game Objective

Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over

Blackjack (21) pays 2.5x your bet!

Card Values

Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10

Ace = 1 or 11 (automatically chosen)

Number cards = their face value

How to Play

  1. 1. Enter your bet amount (₹100 - ₹5000)
  2. 2. Click "PLACE BET" (can click multiple times to increase bet)
  3. 3. Click "START GAME" to begin
  4. 4. Choose your move:
    • "HIT" - Take another card
    • "STAND" - Keep your cards
  5. 5. Win conditions:
    • Get Blackjack (21) = Win 2.5x bet
    • Beat dealer's hand = Win 2x bet
    • Dealer busts = Win 2x bet
    • Tie = Get your bet back

Dealer Rules

Dealer must hit on 16 or below

Dealer must stand on 17 or above
